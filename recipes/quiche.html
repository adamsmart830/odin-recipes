Quiche

This is an easy, delicious quiche that takes no time to prpare using a frozen deep-dish pie crust, bacon bits, cream, and two types of cheese!

Ingredients

  • Pie Crust
  • Bacon
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Cream
    •

    Recipe

    1. Place the frozen pie crust on a baking sheet
    2. Mix the bacon, onion, and cheeses together and pour mixture into the pie crust
    3. In a separate bowl mix the eggs with the half-and-half.
    4. Pour the egg mixture over the cheese-bacon-mixture
    5. Bake in a preeated oven at 400 degrees F for 15 minutes
    6. Reduce heat to 350 degrees F for 35 minutes
    7. Let cool and enjoy!
