Egg Salad Sandwich Recipe

Make deli-worthy egg salad sandwiches at home with this top-rated egg salad recipe! You'll come back to it again and again.

Ingredients

  • 8 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onion
  • 1 tsp prepared yellow mustard
  • 1/4 tsp paprika
  • salt and pepper to taste
    •

    Steps

    1. Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water.
    2. Bring water to a boil and immediately remove from heat
    3. Cover and let eggs stand in ot water for 10 to 12 minutes
    4. Remove from ot water, cool, peel, and chop
    5. Place chopped eggs in a bowl; stir in mayonnaise, green onion, and mustard
    6. Season with paprika, salt and pepper
    7. Stir and serve on your favorite bread or crackers
