Egg Muffin Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 poud bulk pork sausage
  • 12 egs
  • 1/2 cup choped onions
  • 1/2 up chopped green bell pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup chredded Cheddar Cheese
    •

    Steps

    1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
    2. Lightly grease 12 muffin cups or line with paper muffin liners.
    3. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    4. Stir in sausage and cook until crumbly, evenly browned, and no longer pink, 10 to 15 minutes.
    5. Drain off grease
    6. Beat eggs in a large bowl
    7. Stir in onions, green pepper, salt, pepper, and garlic powder
    8. Mix in sausage an Cheddar cheese.
    9. Spoon into the prepared muffin cups
    10. Bake in the preheated oven until a knife insearted near the center comes out clean, 20-25 mins
